About

I finished my undergraduate degree in Physics at NYU Abu Dhabi (2016) and am currently an Assistant Instructor of Physics there. In this role I act as a teaching assistant for the physics components of the 3-semester long Foundations of Science course. I mainly TA for the third-semester component (FoS 5&6) which covers material similar to Physics III at other institutions (waves). My research interests are in applying computational data analysis techniques to astronomy data. One of the projects I'm currently working on is to develop a Python package that efficiently obtains background-corrected light curves from the Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite (TESS) full-frame images!
